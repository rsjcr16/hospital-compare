## Clinical Philosophy

### Approach to Complex Cases
Dr. Rice specializes in patients other surgeons may consider inoperable. His approach includes:
- Comprehensive multidisciplinary evaluation
- Optimal sequencing of treatments
- Aggressive surgery when oncologically appropriate
- Management of complex post-treatment anatomy
- Focus on achieving complete resection

### VATS Wedge Resection in Complex Cases
- Often part of larger treatment plan
- May follow chemotherapy or immunotherapy
- Careful assessment of tissue planes post-treatment
- Readiness to convert to open if needed for safety
