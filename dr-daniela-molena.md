## Clinical Philosophy & Approach

### Patient Care Philosophy
Dr. Molena emphasizes personalized, minimally invasive approaches that optimize both oncologic outcomes and quality of life. Her principles include:
- Minimizing surgical trauma through refined techniques
- Comprehensive pre-operative patient education
- Aggressive pain management with opioid-sparing protocols
- Early mobilization and rapid recovery
- Empowering patients throughout their journey

### Approach to VATS Wedge Resection
1. **Patient Selection**: Careful evaluation of tumor characteristics and patient fitness
2. **Pre-operative Planning**: CT reconstruction, surgical simulation
3. **Technique**: Refined 3-port VATS or robotic approach based on case
4. **Intraoperative**: Real-time frozen section, adequate margin verification
5. **Recovery**: ERAS protocol with same-day mobilization

### Robotic Surgery Integration
- Selective use based on tumor location and patient anatomy
- Training program for fellows in robotic techniques
- Evidence-based approach to technology adoption
