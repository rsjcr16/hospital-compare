## Clinical Philosophy

### Enhanced Recovery Philosophy
Dr. Hofstetter believes surgery should be just one component of a comprehensive recovery program:
- Prepare patients pre-operatively for success
- Minimize surgical trauma through refined techniques
- Aggressive multimodal pain control
- Early mobilization and nutrition
- Empower patients as active participants in recovery

### VATS Wedge Resection with ERAS
1. **Pre-operative Phase**
   - Patient education about ERAS pathway
   - Optimization of comorbidities
   - Pre-operative breathing exercises
   - Nutritional assessment and support

2. **Surgical Phase**
   - Refined minimally invasive technique
   - Strategic port placement to minimize pain
   - Intraoperative analgesia protocols
   - Minimal chest tube use when possible

3. **Recovery Phase**
   - Multimodal pain management (NSAIDs, regional blocks)
   - Mobilization within 4 hours of surgery
   - Early oral intake
   - Structured physical therapy
   - Goal-directed discharge planning
