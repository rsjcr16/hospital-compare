## Clinical Philosophy & Approach

### Patient Care Philosophy
Dr. Jones believes in aggressive, evidence-based treatment for carefully selected patients. His approach emphasizes:
- Comprehensive multidisciplinary evaluation for all patients
- Individualized treatment plans based on tumor biology and patient factors
- Pursuit of cure even in advanced disease when appropriate
- Integration of cutting-edge research into clinical practice
- Compassionate, patient-centered communication

### Approach to VATS Wedge Resection
- **Indications**: Early-stage peripheral lesions, limited pulmonary reserve patients
- **Technique**: Standard 3-port approach with strategic port placement
- **Technology**: Selective use of electromagnetic navigation and CT localization
- **Margin Assessment**: Routine frozen section analysis
- **Recovery**: Standardized enhanced recovery protocol

### Management of Complex Cases
- Leads multidisciplinary tumor board discussions
- Coordinates neoadjuvant therapy when indicated
- Performs extended resections when oncologically necessary
- Provides second opinions for challenging cases worldwide
