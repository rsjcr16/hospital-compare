## Clinical Philosophy

### Approach to Care
Dr. Mehran emphasizes evidence-based surgical approaches with a focus on minimizing patient trauma while achieving excellent oncologic outcomes. His approach includes:
- Comprehensive multidisciplinary evaluation
- Appropriate selection of minimally invasive techniques
- Integration with systemic therapy when indicated
- Focus on rapid recovery and return to quality of life

### VATS Wedge Resection Approach
- Careful patient selection based on tumor location and characteristics
- Standard 3-port VATS technique
- Frozen section margin assessment
- Enhanced recovery protocols
- Structured follow-up
