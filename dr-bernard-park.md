## Clinical Philosophy & Approach

### Patient Care Philosophy

Dr. Park specializes in providing the most appropriate, least invasive surgical approach for early lung cancer and small nodules. His philosophy includes:

- Tailoring extent of resection to tumor characteristics
- Minimizing surgical trauma while achieving oncologic adequacy
- Utilizing advanced localization for challenging nodules
- Providing clear, evidence-based recommendations
- Patient education on surveillance vs. intervention

### Approach to VATS Wedge Resection

1. **Pre-operative Assessment**
   - Detailed CT analysis of tumor location and characteristics
   - Assessment of nodule density and invasiveness
   - Discussion of resection options (wedge vs. segmentectomy vs. lobectomy)

2. **Localization Strategy**
   - CT-guided localization for deep or small nodules
   - Electromagnetic navigation when appropriate
   - Intraoperative ultrasound as backup

3. **Surgical Technique**
   - Utility incision or 3-port VATS based on location
   - Adequate margin assessment (minimum 1cm or tumor diameter)
   - Frozen section analysis for all specimens

4. **Margin Management**
   - Intraoperative margin assessment
   - Immediate re-resection if inadequate
   - Discussion with patient pre-operatively about conversion criteria
