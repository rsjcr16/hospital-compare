## Clinical Philosophy & Approach

### Patient Care Philosophy
Dr. Adusumilli emphasizes individualized treatment plans that integrate the latest research with proven surgical techniques. He believes in:
- Comprehensive preoperative evaluation and optimization
- Minimally invasive approaches when oncologically appropriate
- Integration of immunotherapy and other systemic therapies
- Strong multidisciplinary collaboration
- Patient education and shared decision-making

### Approach to VATS Wedge Resection
1. **Patient Selection**: Careful evaluation of tumor location, size, and patient factors
2. **Preoperative Planning**: CT-guided localization for small nodules when needed
3. **Surgical Technique**: Standard 3-port VATS with selective use of robotic assistance
4. **Margin Assessment**: Intraoperative frozen section for all specimens
5. **Recovery Protocol**: Enhanced recovery pathway with early mobilization
